Annihilator Quest

Introduction

This page discusses the Annihilator quest. An infamously difficult test of strength, skill and luck.

Damage Analysis - set up

The quest must be completed by four players level 100 and above. There are 6 demons, and each of the four players can be hit by up to 3 energy beams.

Demons cast fireballs dealing 110-200 fire damage with a chance of 1/3, and energy damage beams of damage 300-420 with a chance of 1/10. Due to CIP code, the 5 demons that are not in melee range will roll the 1/3 and 1/10 chance to cast every second and the remaining demon every two seconds.

The average damage on turn 1 and 2 is therefore 310 + 108 = 418, and 258 + 72 = 330, for an overall average of 374 damage.
