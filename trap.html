by Tarsal.

Traps


  1. Traps can be bought in Edron for 280gp a piece. They do a fixed 30 damage, reduced by armour.
  2. You put them on the ground and right click to set (open). If a monster walks over it, it will do damage. If you walk over it, it will close and do no damage. If it is set under a player or monster it will stay open.
  3. The easiest way to use is to place and open a trap under your character with a monster adjacent, then take a step away in the opposite direction. The monster will follow and step on the trap. Then drag the trap back under the character and repeat quickly.
  4. The speed between hits depends on monster, terrain, etc but can be less than every 2 seconds.
  5. Although underused in 7.4 era, they have a great potential to add DPS.
  6. Monster armour can greatly reduce trap damage which leads to their being inneffective against 25+ armour opponents.
  7. You will get zero experience if you only deal trap damage. You will gain full experience if you do any other damage - so one light magic missile shot would be sufficient to get full experience. Mages can dispatch minotaur guards and similar monsters in this way.
