Traps


  1. Traps can be bought in Edron for 280gp a piece, and weighs a modest 21oz. They deal a fixed 30 damage, reduced by armor.
  2. To use traps sinoly place them on the ground and right click to set (open). If a monster or player walks on or is pushed onto a trap, it will close, dealing damage to monsters but not players. If a player or monster is already on a trap before it was set or subsequently walks off it will stay open.
  3. The easiest way to use is to place and open a trap under your character with a monster adjacent, then take a step away in the opposite direction. The monster will follow and step on the trap. Then drag the trap back under the character and repeat quickly.
  4. The rate that a monster can be hit can be every 2 seconds or less.
  5. Although underused in 7.4 era, they have a good potential to increase your damage output.
  6. Monster armor can greatly reduce trap damage which leads to their being inneffective against 25+ armour opponents.
  7. To gain full experience from the monster killed with traps you will need to deal 1 or more damage from another source like physical attack or spell. For example a mage can kill a minotaur guard with one light magic missile shot, traps and get full experience.
  8. Multiple traps can be used, with the more ambitous using 8 in a square around an obstacle to deal continuous damage while staying out of range.
