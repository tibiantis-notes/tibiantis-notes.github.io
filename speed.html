Speed

This page describes the mechanics of speed in Tibia 7.4.

Base speed

Base speed is 220 at level 1 and increases by 2 per level:
 BaseSpeed = 218 + (Level * 2)

Speed modifying items


Boots of haste increase speed by 40 (equivalent to 20 levels).


Time rings increase speed by 60 (equivalent to 30 levels).

Time rings lasts for 10 minutes.

Winged helmet doesn't affect speed.

Haste

Haste (Utani Hur) increases speed as follows:
 ModifiedSpeed = (BaseSpeed * 1.3) - 24

Strong Haste (Utani Gran Hur) increases speed as follows:
 ModifiedSpeed = (BaseSpeed * 1.7) - 56

Then resulting value is rounded down (to be confirmed).

Paralyze


The Paralyze rune is exclusive to (Elder?) Druid and costs 600 mana to make and a further 600 to use. It sets speed to 40, cancelling haste and overriding base speed.
 
ModifiedSpeed = 40
A diagonal step on the slowest tile can take as long as 15 seconds to complete under paralysis. During a step you cannot use runes although instant spells can be used.

Speed formula and breakpoints

 TotalSpeed = ModifiedSpeed + ItemModifier

 StepTime = roundup(TerrainFactor / TotalSpeed, 0.05)

The time for each step, in seconds, is speed divided by the terrain factor, then rounded to the nearest 50 milliseconds. The rounding can have a significant impact on speed causing noticable jumps at certain 'breakpoints'. The higher the speed, the more spreadout but impactful these breakpoints become.
Taken to the extreme a level 300 player using strong haste, BoH and a time ring will have a speed of 1434, on a fast terrain this would be a step time of 100/1434 = roundup(0.0697,0.05) = 0.010. This is a 43% less than expected.

 DiagonalStepTime = 3 * step time

Using Pythagoras a digonal step should take 1.41x longer than an orthogonal one, but instead it takes 3x! This is not an efficient way to move except to avoid fire fields and in PvP.
